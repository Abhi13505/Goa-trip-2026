Tips for the Crew

A few things to keep the trip smooth, safe, and full of good vibes.

Transportation

Rent scooters or bikes for easy navigation, especially in North Goa. Taxis are also available.

Stay Hydrated

Goa gets hot and humid — drink plenty of water and tender coconut water.

Sun Protection

Carry sunscreen, hats, and sunglasses for those long beach days.

Carry Cash

Cards are widely accepted, but keep cash handy for shacks and small purchases.

Safety

Keep an eye on your belongings, especially on crowded beaches and in markets.

Embrace the Vibe

Goa is all about relaxation and fun. Go with the flow and enjoy every moment.
